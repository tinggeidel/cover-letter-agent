ExampleCo is a Series B AI company building agents that automate back-office work for mid-market services businesses. We have 40 employees, are remote-first with an HQ in San Francisco, and our customers include three Fortune 500 companies running pilots.

About the role:

- Design and ship AI agents in Python that integrate with the customer's systems
- Own the operational metrics those agents move
- Bring learnings back to the core product team

What we're looking for:
- 3+ years shipping production software
- Comfort with Python and modern LLM tooling (Anthropic SDK, OpenAI SDK, LangChain, etc.)
- Experience explaining technical decisions to non-technical operators
- Hunger to be at the messy intersection of customer and product
- Bonus: experience building agents in production
- Bonus: experience in services-business operations
